The Restraint Indicator field (position 22) identifies the restraint system present in the vehicle according to the information
4
available from the vehicle’s manufacturer.
5
6
An active restraint is one that requires the occupant's participation to engage the device (e.g. standard seat belts). A passive
7
restraint system engages itself around the occupant. Passive Restraints may be either motorized or non-motorized. A
8
motorized passive restraint means that a motor positions the belt about the occupant. A non-motorized passive restraint is
9
positioned about the occupant, without a motor, when the vehicle's door is closed.
10
11
A blank in this field does not mean that the vehicle does not have one of the restraint systems described above. It means that
12
the manufacturer of the vehicle did not provide us with the information necessary to determine the specific type of restraint
13
system.

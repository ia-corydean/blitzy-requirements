Since a vehicle's Symbol can change over time, the Effective Date (positions 18-21) is used to identify which Symbol
4
is effective for the policy being written. The correct Symbol is contained on the record with the latest effective
5
date which is on or before the policy’s effective date.
6
7
A VIN listed with an Effective Date will always have at least one entry listing a prior Symbol. For example, consider
8
a vehicle that is re-symbolled in each of the four annual VSR reviews for the vehicle’s model year. The VIN for this
9
vehicle will appear on five records. The first record will have a blank Effective Date and the introductory Symbol.
10
The second record will have an Effective Date of MMYY, as described below, and the first Symbol update. The third
11
record will have an Effective Date of MMAA, as described below, and the second Symbol update. The fourth record
12
will have an Effective Date of MMBB, as described below, and the third Symbol update. The fifth record will have
13
an Effective Date of MMZZ, as described below, and the final Symbol for that vehicle.
14
15
NOTE: For model years 1986 through 2007, vehicles only went through two annual VSR reviews and so, there would
16
at most be three different effective date records per VIN for these model years.
17
18
EXCEPTION: Additionally, the 2022 VSR Review (released in calendar year 2023) will be an exception to the rules below.
19
Model years 2019-2022 will have an effective date of 0423 for the records pertaining to the 2022 VSR Review.
20
21
Possible values for Effective Date Fields are:
22
23
BlankIndicates that the accompanying Symbol was the initial Symbol assigned to the vehicle. For some new models, the initial Symbol is updated based on later information from the manufacturer. This revised Symbol will replace the original Symbol on the record with the blank Effective Date, and the Symbol Change Indicator (position 29) for that update will be assigned in accordance with the Symbol Change Indicator table.
24
MMYY(MM = Month of the VSR Review. YY = Model Year of the vehicle.) Indicates that the accompanying Symbol was assigned to the vehicle at the first annual VSR Review for that model year.
25
MMAA(MM = Month of the VSR Review. AA = Model Year + 1 of the vehicle.) Indicates that the accompanying Symbol was assigned to the vehicle at the second annual VSR Review for that model year.
26
MMBB(MM = Month of the VSR Review. BB = Model Year + 2 of the vehicle.) Indicates that the accompanying Symbol was assigned to the vehicle at the third annual VSR Review for that model year.
27
MMZZ(MM = Month of the VSR Review. ZZ= Model Year + 3 of the vehicle.) Indicates that the accompanying Symbol was assigned to the vehicle at the final annual VSR Review for that model year.

Multiple Effective Date Search
30
31
For a VIN with multiple effective dates, please follow the instructions below in order to choose the correct VSR symbols:
32
33
1. Extract the Policy Effective Date from your files.
34
35
2. Compare the Policy Effective Date to the Effective Date field on the VINMASTER® records to find the record which
36
applies. Note that each vehicle may be adjusted by any of the 4 annual VSR reviews in its lifetime.
37
38
3. Search the Effective Date field for a MMZZ entry. If such an entry exists and if the Policy Effective Date is on or
39
after MMZZ, then use that record.
40
41
4. If the Policy Effective Date is prior to MMZZ, or VINMASTER® does not contain a MMZZ entry, then search for a
42
MMBB entry. If such an entry exists and if the Policy Effective Date is on or after MMBB, then use that record.
43
44
5. If the Policy Effective Date is prior to MMBB, or VINMASTER® does not contain a MMBB entry, then search the
45
Effective Date field for a MMAA entry. If such an entry exists and if the Policy Effective Date is on or after MMAA,
46
then use that record.
47
48
6. If the Policy Effective Date is prior to MMAA, or VINMASTER® does not contain a MMAA entry, then search the
49
Effective Date field for a MMYY entry. If such an entry exists and if the Policy Effective Date is on or after MMYY,
50
then use that record.
51
52
7. If the Policy Effective Date is prior to MMYY, or VINMASTER® does not contain a MMYY entry, then search the
53
Effective Date field for a blank entry. Use that record.

Position 10 of the VIN indicates the model year of the vehicle. A two-position numeric model year
4
is also provided (positions 11-12). It consists of the last two positions of the year (i.e. 2012 = 12).

Please note that for Model Year 1980, the standard VIN had not yet been developed. The 10th
7
position of 1980 models does not consistently represent the model year. In order to determine
8
the model year for a 1980 vehicle, you must refer to position 11-12.

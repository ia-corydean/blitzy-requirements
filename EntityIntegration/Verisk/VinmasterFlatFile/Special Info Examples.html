4
Consider a vehicle with a given Model Name, Body Style, and Engine Information, but the vehicle is
5
available with either an automatic transmission or a manual transmission. If these vehicles differed
6
in Symbol, we would differentiate them with the Special Information fields.
7
8
In this example, a code of T will appear in position 121 for both vehicles since they both require
9
Transmission Information to make the distinction. "AUTO" will appear in the Transmission Special
10
Information field for the vehicle with an automatic transmission and, "MAN" will appear in the
11
Transmission Special Information field for the vehicle with a manual transmission. To locate the
12
correct Symbol, in addition to Model Name, Body Style and Engine Information, the "search" must
13
include the Transmission Special Information field.

EXAMPLE 2:
22
Consider four vehicles with the same Model Name, Body Style, and Engine Information, but the vehicle
23
is available with either an automatic transmission or a manual transmission and a Long or Short Bed.
24
25
In this example, a code of A will appear in position 121 of all four vehicles to indicate that Transmission
26
and Body special information is required. "AUTO" will appear in the Transmission Special Information
27
field for the vehicles with an automatic transmission, and "MAN" will appear in the Transmission
28
Special Information field for the vehicles with a manual transmission. Also, "L/B" will appear in the
29
Body Special Information field for the vehicles with a Long Bed, and "S/B" will appear in the Body
30
Special Information field for the vehicles with a Short Bed.

EXAMPLE 4:
55
In rare cases, the Special Information distinction involves more than one Special Information Selector
56
value.
57
58
To get the correct Symbol in these cases, first use the distinction that involves a single type of Special
59
Information. These are records with Selectors equal to B, E, M, O, R or T. If the vehicle has the
60
characteristic indicated, then the Symbol will appear on one of these records. If not, you will then
61
use the records with the other Selector to find the right Symbol.
62
63
In this example, you would first use the distinction contained on the record with Special Information
64
Selector O. If the vehicle had a Navigation System, its Symbol would be C. If it does not have a
65
Navigation System, you would use the records with Special Information Selector J. If the vehicle is
66
a Premium model, its Symbol would be C. If it is not a Premium model, its Symbol would be A.

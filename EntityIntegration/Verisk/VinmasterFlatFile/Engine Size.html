The Engine Size field (positions 58-60) specifies the size of the engine.

For Model Years prior to 1991, this field specifies the size of the engine in Cubic Inch Displacement
6

For Model Years 1991 and subsequent, this field specifies the size of the engine in Liters (L).
10
The three-position field will be in the form "#.#". That is to say, the field will have a decimal
11
point in the second position (e.g., an engine size of "2.0" represents a 2.0 liter engine).

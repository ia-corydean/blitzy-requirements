The Special Information fields provide vehicle characteristics that are used to differentiate vehicles when performing a search based
4
on the vehicle description, and not the VIN. In cases where the Model Name, Body Style (including doors), and Engine information are
5
not sufficient to identify a unique Symbol, Special Information is required to make this distinction.

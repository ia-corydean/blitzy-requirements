The Payload Capacity field (positions 68-71) is a four-position field containing the payload
4
capacity of the vehicle in pounds. Payload Capacity includes the weight of the driver,
5
passengers, optional equipment, and cargo. This calculation is performed for Class Codes
6
91 (Small Utility), 93 (Large Utility), 81 (Small Pickup), 83 (Large Pickup), and 70 (Van).
7
8
Where the gross vehicle weight is 00000 or where the Class Code is not 70, 81, 83, 91 or 93,
9
the Payload Capacity will be 0000.

Beginning with the 1981 model year, the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration,
4
Department of Transportation, required manufacturers selling over the road vehicles
5
in the United States to provide the vehicles with a 17-character vehicle identification
6
number (VIN).
7
8
This standard establishes a fixed VIN format, including a check-digit, and applies to all
9
passenger cars, multi-purpose vehicles, trucks, buses, trailers, incomplete vehicles,
10
and motorcycles with a gross vehicle weight of 10,000 pounds or less.
11
12

14
steps.

Check-Digit Exceptions
83
Certain 2015 Jeep Renegade and 2016 Fiat 500x VINs were issued that compute as invalid.
84
Issues with the check-digit are beyond the scope of what VINMASTER® can verify with the
85
manufacturer. If you have confirmed the VIN is correct with the customer and you were
86
able to find the VIN on VINMASTER®, then we suggest you use the information provided on
87
VINMASTER® to insure the vehicle.  If you are not able to find the confirmed VIN on
88
VINMASTER® because it hasn’t been symbolled yet, then you would use your typical
89
procedure set in place at your company for handling vehicles not yet symbolled, such as
90
the Interim Symbol Rule if your company has filed it.

VINMASTER® is a database designed to be used with customer-written software in systems that use ISO Personal Auto Symbols.
4
VINMASTER® allows you to locate the correct ISO Statistical Symbols, as well as other relevant insurance information, for vehicles
5
that are contained in the ISO Symbol and Identification Manual. For companies writing business in jurisdictions in which the ISO
6
Vehicle Series Rating (VSR) Symbols have been approved for their use, these Symbols may be used to rate the Physical Damage
7
portion of the policy. This document outlines the specifications for software development that should allow you to use
8
VINMASTER® to either verify or assign the correct ISO Symbol.
9
10
VINMASTER® allows you to search the database by either VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) or by the Model Name/Description
11
of the vehicle.
12
13
VINMASTER® Physical Damage is designed to agree with the information provided in the ISO Personal Auto Symbol and Identification
14
Manual. Its release schedule is soon after each update of that manual.

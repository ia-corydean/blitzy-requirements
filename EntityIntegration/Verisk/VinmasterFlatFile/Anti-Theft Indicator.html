The Anti-Theft Indicator field (position 166) identifies whether the vehicle has been equipped with a
4
factory installed Anti-Theft Device.
5
6
An active Anti-Theft Device is one where manual intervention is required in order to activate the device.
7
A passive Anti-Theft Device requires no manual intervention in order for the device to be activated.
8

Please be aware: The Anti-Theft information identifies only those standard and optional Anti-Theft
25
Devices (ATDs) that are factory-installed by the original equipment manufacturer (OEM).  Information is not
26
provided for ATDs installed by non-OEMs (e.g., auto dealers, auto security firms) because that information
27
is not available to us.  In most states, ATD premium discount requirements generally do not limit discount
28
eligibility to OEM-installed ATDs.  You are cautioned to review applicable state requirements and to use the
29
information accordingly.

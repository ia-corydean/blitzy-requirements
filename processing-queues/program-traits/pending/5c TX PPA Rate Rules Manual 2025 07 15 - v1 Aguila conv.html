Multi-Car Discount - A discount will apply to policies with multiple vehicles.
Prior Insurance Discount - A prior insurance discount will apply if the applicant has maintained
continuous private passenger auto insurance for at least a six (6) month period and has not been
lapsed for more than thirty (30) days.
Applicants should be rated without this discount unless adequate proof of prior insurance is
submitted with the application. If the proof is to be provided during the policy term, the company will
apply the discount to the policy the date the proof is received.
The only acceptable form of proof for the "prior coverage" discount are, a copy of the prior
company issued I.D. Card, Dec Page, Current Billing Notice, Letter of Experience, Renewal
Notice, Non-Renewal Notice, Agency Transfer and Underwriting Report. The proof of prior
insurance must be in the name of the insured or he/she must be a listed driver on the prior policy.
The proof of discount must be uploaded to MGA.com for it to be used in the rating of the policy. On
a new policy, an MGA policy may not be used as the proof of prior insurance unless the Named
Insured on the new policy:
A. Was a ‘parent’ or ‘child’ of the Named Insured on the policy that is being used for the
proof of prior insurance discount?
B. Was a divorced/separated ‘spouse’ of the Named Insured on the policy that is being used
for the proof of prior insurance?
C. Was the Named Insured on a policy that cancelled or lapsed due to overseas military
deployment?
D. Was the Named Insured/Spouse on a policy that was continuously in force with MGA for
at least six (6) months and has canceled/lapsed for 0-30 days and is being rewritten
through website rewrite process?
1. When the policy is being rewritten due to lapse or cancellation, the prior discount
code that was on the policy prior to cancellation will be used. We do not allow
the agent to re-shop the rate with MGA to provide a different discount code that
may give a lower rate than currently offered.
Homeowner Discount - A discount will apply if the named insured owns a home. Home
ownership includes condominiums, town homes, modular and mobile homes if the insured also
owns the land that the modular or mobile home occupies. Proof of home ownership (i.e. copy of
homeowner insurance declarations page, property tax assessment or mortgage payment coupon)
must accompany the application for discount to apply to new business. If insured changes address
during the policy term, new proof of ownership for the new residence is required. The insured’s
name and property address on the documentation showing ownership must be the same as the
insured’s name and mailing address on our policy. The Named Insured must reside in the home
and be a Listed Driver. Renewal customers can also apply for this discount by submitting any of
the above documentation.

Eligible Vehicles
Private passenger automobiles, sport utility vehicles, pickup trucks, and vans are eligible when the load capacity
does not exceed one (1) ton, the gross vehicle weight does not exceed 10,000 pounds and all other eligibility
requirements are met. All vehicles should be titled to the named insured at the time of binding new business or a
policy level surcharge will apply until all vehicles are title to the named insured.

Driver Class
•
Driver Class is defined by marital status (Married, Single), Gender (Male, Female), Driver Age (adjusted
at the inception of each policy term).
•
The spouse of each married driver must be included or excluded as a driver on the policy.
•
Classifications rated as Single when Single, Divorced, Separated, Common Law. Rated as Married when
Married if both individuals are listed Drivers, Widowed or Excluded.
•
When there are more vehicles than drivers on a policy, the “URD” driver class rate will apply to vehicles
without a driver assigned.
Driver’s License Status
New residents moving into Texas have ninety (90) days after entry into the state to secure a Texas Drivers’
License. Military Personnel are not required to obtain a Texas Driver’s License. Photo ID of the Named Insured is
required to be uploaded to the system. The matrix of factor is detailed in the Rate Specifications
The following are acceptable, and a surcharge may apply.
Texas DL
- No surcharge is applied to drivers who present a valid Texas driver’s license issued for at least six
(6) months. Military Personnel are not required to obtain a Texas Driver’s License. “TEXAS” refers to a driver’s
license issued by the State of Texas.
Texas ID
- The “Texas ID” surcharge will be rated at Renewal with a “Texas DL” if a Valid Texas DL is
obtained during the policy period.
US DL, State other than Texas
- The “US DL” surcharge will be rated at Renewal with a “Texas DL” if a
valid Texas DL is obtained during the policy period.
Foreign DL / International DL
- The “International” surcharge will be rated at renewal with a “Texas DL” if a
Valid Texas DL is obtained during the policy period. “INTERNATIONAL” refers to a permit issued by a
government-authorized organization allowing the driver to drive in foreign countries by verifying license
information.
Matricula
- The “Matricula” surcharge will be rated at Renewal with a “Texas DL” if a Valid Texas DL is
obtained during the policy period.
Passport –
The “Passport” surcharge will be rated at Renewal with a “Texas DL” if a Valid Texas DL is
obtained during the policy period.
No Driver’s License
- The “No Driver’s License” is not eligible for New Business. At Renewal, a Driver
whose license has expired during the prior term will be rated as “No Driver’s License”.
Exclusion of Named Driver
All persons in the insured’s household age 15 or older must be listed on the application as a driver; otherwise,
those persons must be excluded with endorsement 515A. The named insured may not be excluded. All married
insured’s must give driver information for their spouse in the driver section of the application. They may exclude
their spouse with a signed endorsement 515A. The “Non-Rated Spouse” surcharge will be applied when the
Named Insured excludes their spouse except when the occupation of the excluded spouse is listed as “Military”.
Drivers with a permanently revoked license are unacceptable risks and must be excluded. Suspended (without
SR-22), cancelled, or revoked are unacceptable risks and must be excluded.

Mandatory Endorsements
Policies offered under this program differ from the customary coverage provided under the Texas
Standard Personal Auto Policy.
Mandatory endorsements approved by the Texas Department of Insurance that
significantly change the coverages from the Texas Standard Personal Auto Policy are attached to the policy jacket
and shown by endorsement number on the Declarations page. These Endorsements are NOT
optional. Total
premium shown on the application and on the Declaration page is based in part on the acceptance of these
endorsements. These coverage differences should be explained to the insured prior to binding the policy. A
disclosure form requiring the named insured’s signature summarizes these differences; this form should be
signed, and a copy given to the insured. Coverage cannot be bound unless a signed disclosure form is included in
the submission with the signed application to the company. The program will use the standard Texas PAP and be
modified using the following approved endorsements:
•
OACM.YCA.002c – Newly Acquired Automobile
•
OACM.Storage.004 – Storage
•
OACM.DelFee.005 – Delivery of Persons and Property for a Fee
•
OACM.ContLiab.006 – Contractual Liability Exclusion
•
OACM.AutoTerm.007 – Automatic Termination
•
OACM.CrimeIntent.008 – Criminal or Intentional Acts
•
OACM.MisrepFraud.009 – Misrepresentation or Fraud Endorsement
•
OACM.OutofState.016a – Restricted Out of State Coverage
